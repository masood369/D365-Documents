Business Goals and Objectives
- What are your primary business objectives for implementing an ERP system?
- How do you expect the ERP system to improve your business processes?
- What specific problems or inefficiencies are you currently experiencing that you hope the ERP system will address?

Current Systems and Processes
- What systems and software are you currently using to manage your business operations?
- Can you provide an overview of your current business processes and workflows?
- Are there any specific processes that you feel are particularly inefficient or problematic?

Functional Requirements
- What core functionalities do you need from the ERP system (e.g.SALES, HR, ACCOUNTS)?
- Are there any industry-specific requirements or regulations that the ERP system must comply with?


Technical Requirements
- What are your preferences regarding the deployment model (on-premises, cloud-based, hybrid)?
- What are your security and data privacy requirements?
- Do you have any existing IT infrastructure or hardware considerations we need to take into account?

Integration and Customization:
- Do you have existing systems or software that the ERP system needs to integrate with?
- Are there any specific customization requirements that are essential for your business?

Scope and Scale:
- What is the size and complexity of your organization?
- How many users will need access to the ERP system, and what roles will they have?
- Do you have multiple locations or subsidiaries that need to be integrated into the ERP system?

Budget and Timeline:
- What is your budget for implementing an ERP system?
- What is your desired timeline for the implementation?
- Are there any budget constraints or deadlines that need to be considered?

Data and Security:
- How do you currently manage your data, and what data migration needs do you anticipate?
- What are your data security requirements, and how do you ensure compliance with regulations?


Data and Reporting
- What types of data do you need to manage within the ERP system?
- What are your requirements for data migration from existing systems to the new ERP system?
- What reporting and analytics capabilities are essential for your business?

User Training 
- Who will be the primary users of the ERP system?
- How many users will need access to the system, and what roles will they have?
- What are your training needs for ensuring all users are proficient with the new system?
- How do you plan to train your employees on using the new ERP system?

Change Management
- How do you plan to manage organizational change during the ERP implementation?
- What are your strategies for ensuring employee buy-in and minimizing resistance?
- Do you have a dedicated team or personnel for overseeing the ERP implementation project?

Support and Maintenance:
- What level of ongoing support and maintenance do you expect from the ERP vendor?
- How do you plan to handle upgrades and updates to the ERP system in the future?

Success Metrics:
- How will you measure the success of the ERP implementation?
- What key performance indicators (KPIs) are important for you to track post-implementation?
- How often will you review and assess the performance of the ERP system?
