Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations Learning Repository

This repository contains learning materials related to Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations (D365 F&O). It is intended to support individuals who are looking to build or enhance their understanding of the system from a functional and implementation perspective.

The content shared here is based on practical experience and focuses on real-world business processes, system configuration, and implementation approaches. It includes process guides, sample documents, templates, and notes that can help in understanding how different modules of D365 F&O work together.

This repository is suitable for beginners, functional consultants, and professionals involved in ERP implementations who want structured and practical reference material. The aim is to provide clear and usable content rather than theoretical explanations.

All materials are shared for learning purposes only. Any sensitive or client-specific information has been removed or generalized. The content should be reviewed and adapted based on specific project or business requirements before use in any live environment.

This repository will be updated over time with additional documents and improvements based on ongoing work and learning.
